## Which Mode Should I Use?

| Situation | Use | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Small fix | Normal prompt | Sisyphus can handle it directly. |
| Complex but you are lazy | `ultrawork` or `ulw` | OmO explores, delegates, implements, and verifies. |
| Complex and precise | Prometheus, then `/start-work` | You get an interviewed plan and Atlas-led execution. |

## Recommended First Workflow

```text
ultrawork add the missing test coverage for the payment parser
```

For higher control:

```text
@plan migrate the settings page without changing behavior
/start-work
```

## Model Setup Guidance

Sisyphus works best with Claude Opus-class or Claude-like models such as Kimi K2.5 and GLM 5. Hephaestus and Oracle prefer GPT-5.4-class reasoning. Explore and Librarian should stay on fast, cheap models. Do not override models unless you know why.
