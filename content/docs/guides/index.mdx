## Ultrawork

`ulw` is the “just do it” path. Give a goal and the surface that proves it works.

```text
ulw fix the login error message and verify success/failure in the browser
```

## Prometheus + Atlas

Use this when correctness matters more than speed. Prometheus interviews and plans; Atlas executes the approved plan.

## Model Rule of Thumb

Do not memorize defaults. Use categories. Keep Explore/Librarian cheap and fast; reserve high reasoning models for Sisyphus, Hephaestus, Oracle, and planning.
