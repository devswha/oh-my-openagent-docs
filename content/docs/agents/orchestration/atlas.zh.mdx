Reads Prometheus plans and drives them through the task system. Sonnet by default; dual-prompt with GPT auto-switch via `isGptModel()`.

**Default model:** `claude-sonnet-4-6`.

**Fallback:** Sonnet → Kimi K2.6 → GPT-5.5 (medium) → MiniMax M2.7.

**Cannot delegate** via `task`/`call_omo_agent` — its only fan-out is the task system. `todo-continuation-enforcer` keeps it moving past single-task failures.
