Press **Tab** or `@plan "<task>"` to enter Prometheus mode. Interviews you, grounds questions in the codebase, writes `.sisyphus/plans/*.md`, then `/start-work` activates Atlas.

**Default model:** `claude-opus-4-7` (max). Dual-prompt — Claude or GPT, switched by `isGptModel()` at runtime.

**Fallback:** Opus → GPT-5.5 (high) → GLM-5.1 → Gemini-3.1-pro.

**Constraints:** `prometheus-md-only` hook enforces markdown output. Cannot edit production code.
